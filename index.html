About Red Kite

Red Kite is a spin-out company from the University of Leeds, who specialise in processing and visualizing ESA's Sentinel-1 satellite data.

This demo application provides you with an opportunity to explore the rich data set of InSAR measurements, through a simple interface.

All developments on this website have been produced using open-source software.
